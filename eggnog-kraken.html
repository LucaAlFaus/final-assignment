Kraken Eggnog with Chocolate bitters

Delicious rum based Eggnog, ideal for the Holidays and a warm relaxing night in front of the firepit. The ideal craft of this cocktail follow these steps: dry shake the egg whites, 0.5oz Grand Marnier, 1.5 oz Kraken Rum and 5 drops of chocolate bitters. Add ice and shake again, double strain in a old fashioned glass.
